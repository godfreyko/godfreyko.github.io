Project Management

CIT 380

Week 1 Overview

Outcomes

By the end of the lesson, each student will:

  1. Feel attachment to and explain attributes of their classmates
  2. Recite and explain some of the fundamental aspects of PM
  3. Appreciate the importance of PM principles in professional and everyday life

Overview

Welcome to CIT 380: Project Managment. During this introductory week you will need to familiarize yourself with the expectations of the course, begin learning about Project manangement by reading and thinking about a few project case studies, begin familiarizing yourself with the vocabulary of Project Management, and get an introduction to the PMBOK Guide.

Prepare

  • Carefully read the Syllabus
  • Read chapter 1 in the PMBOK Guide.
  • Review the glossary at the back of the PMBOK Guide
  • Choose and study a case study in preparation for the Icebreaker Activity

Ponder

  • Assess your initial understanding of Project Management Vocabulary by completing the vocabulary quiz.
  • Complete the Icebreaker Activity
  • Begin thinking about which path you are interested in following to complete this course. The paths are outlined in the Syllabus.

Prove

  • Complete the Ch1 PMBOK Reading Quiz
